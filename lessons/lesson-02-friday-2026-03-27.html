1. Vocabulary & Semantics

Actions (Verbes)

To waste: to use inefficiently.
To break down: to decompose.
To skim: to read a text quickly.
To allow / permit / authorise: to let.
To avoid / escape / evade: to stay away.
To trick / cheat: to deceive.
To glance (at): to look quickly (at).
To read out loud: to read with SOUND.

Concepts (Noms & Adverbes)

Workload: amount of work.
Worth: value.
Straight: direct.
Anchor: device/method to restrict motion or fix attention.
Must: (moral) obligation.
Forever: always.
Loud: noisy / high in volume.
Chunk: large piece.
Alongside: by the side / beside.
Along: through.

Abstracts & Synthesis

Summary / Recapitulation: brief account of main points.
Synthesis: merging entities into a single unified entity.
Own: relating to / belonging to oneself.
Highlighter: fluorescent marker pen.
