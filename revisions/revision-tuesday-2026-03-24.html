Phonetics & Learning Strategy

I. The PQ4R Method (Mental Workflow)

Order is logic. Mathias must master these 6 steps to optimize his "Workload":

  1. Preview: Skim the text for headers/diagrams.
  2. Question: Turn headers into questions.
  3. Read: Search for answers to your questions.
  4. Reflect: Think of examples and mental "Anchors".
  5. Recite: State the answers out loud (Sound is key).
  6. Review: Summarize the main points.
