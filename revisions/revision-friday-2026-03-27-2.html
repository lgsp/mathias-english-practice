I. Thomas Sowell: Economics vs. Politics

Quotes from the 3 videos analyzed for Mathias' logical training:

"There are no solutions, there are only trade-offs."
Concept: Optimization. You can't have everything, you must choose the best ratio.
"The first lesson of economics is scarcity. The first lesson of politics is to disregard the first lesson of economics."
Vocabulary: Scarcity (Rareté) vs. Disregard (Ignorer).
"It is amazing how much panic one honest man can spread among a multitude of hypocrites."
"The problem isn't that Johnny can't read... he confuses thinking with feeling."

II. The "Efficiency" Protocol

Effective: Success based on the Result. (Killing a fly with a sledgehammer).

Efficient: Success based on Resources. (Killing a fly with a flyswatter).

Vocabulary from notes: Worth (valeur), Waste (gaspiller), Workload (charge de travail).
