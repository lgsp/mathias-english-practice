I. Essential Vocabulary

Acquisition The process of developing a skill or habit.
Fluency The ability to speak or write a language easily and accurately.
Comprehensible Able to be understood; intelligible.
Shadowing A technique where you repeat what a speaker says as they say it.
Solidify To make a concept or memory stronger and more certain.
Reinforce To strengthen an existing idea or habit.
Customizable Able to be modified to suit a specific person's needs.
Vlog A personal video blog recording a person's life or thoughts.
Acquire To buy or obtain an object or asset.
Tense A grammatical category which marks the time of an action (past, present, etc.).
